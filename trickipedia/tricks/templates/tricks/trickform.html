rules

each skier can only be featured in the trickipedia up to 2 times
we will only accept submissions for tricks that aren't already in the trickipedia
if we are reopening submissions for a certain trick it will be stated on that trick's page
you must supply the youtube embed code for your video, not the url
videos must have no more than 2 seconds of deadspace before and after the trick
video footage must be clear and the skier's body must remain in frame
videos must have only raw skiing audio. any submissions with music will not be considered
audio is not required
tricks must be executed and landed very well. no shuffles, no reverts
if you grab a trick grabs must be held. no touches or missed grabs
only named switch up to 270 out combos (ie: k-fed, illusive) will be accepted
providing your social links is encouraged but not required
